What is Astralship?

Astralship is a project to create visionary facility for Creativity, Innovation and Collaboration. We will leverage state of the art developments in fields from Architecture and IoT, to Psychology and CNC manufacturing. We'll repurpose an old Chapel in Snowdonia and create a space that enables teams reach their full potential, and change the world.

Pirate ships and Innovation

We're building a facility where developers, designers, engineers and radical thinkers work together in flow states uninterupted by the outside world. Steve Jobs said he'd rather be a Pirate than join the navy. We too, and we like naval metaphores for innovation. Innovators don't accept the status quo, they go out and change it. We're building a ship that provides everything a crew needs to go on deep innovation journeys. An optimal physical and psychological space, for channel passion, tackling the hard problems, and changing the world.

Total Immersion

  • No Barriers: All immediate physical and psychological needs of the crew met
  • No distractions: An inspiring and self contained world to sustain and shelter the crew from the everyday world
  • Space and support: Space to decompress, relax and hike when the energies get too high

A inspiring and exciting space

In the Chapel there is plenty of room to work with. We've already built a prototype ship and found it to be an inspirational space. Now we want to upgrade and share it with the world.

Innovation is an adventure. Here we'll create a magical and inspiring interior with high-quality living, working and recreational spaces that suggest adventure on even the most serious and focussed of voyages.

The Voyages

We're building a ship, but once built where will we sail? We have different revenue models; sometimes we'll assemble specialist crews to tackle our innovation challenges, sometimes we'll lease it out entirely. The ship will allow the bravest and brightest to push themselves to their limits. Some voyages will be very intense, lasting up to 2 weeks, and some crews will need specialist facilitation to reach their goals. On a voyage, startup teams will design and prototype new products or services, work that might have taken many months under normal condition.

Stunning Natural Location

The Chapel stands on the slopes of Elidir across the valley from Snowdon (the highest mountain in England and Wales) a few hundred metres from the national park.

When tackling a hard problem, sometimes the best thing to do is take a break and get some altitude. Snowdonia, and it’s breathtaking scenery of mountains and beaches is literally on the doorstep.

After a hike in the middle of a challenging voyage, there will be an Japanese onsen style hot-tub to unwind in, and a hearty meal before the nexts days work begins.

Let's go sailing

For now we are gathering interest in the project and looking for those who want to come on a journey with us.

We are planning to launch a crowdfunding campaign to get additional funds, gather momentum and gauge interest in our project. See our investment proposal.

  • Building the ship

    At minimum need a new floor, insulation, ligtning, a heating system and a inspiring interior (sorry this is essential). We estimate we need around £50,000 to retrofit our prototype. We'll architect things in such away that we can continue to build on and enhance the space in future. The money will come from our savings, family and friends, and crowding funding. We're developing this plan now and are looking architects, artists, psychologists, carpenters, builders and creators of all kinds who want to be involved.

  • Sharing the wealth created

    In the convential paradigm, getting a team together to work on ideas is expensive and time consuming. The big money investors decide on the projects and keep the rewards. By providing a ship which is able to sustain teams at low cost, teams can reach prototype stage with optimal efficiency, minimising the time (and funds)the founders need to reach fruition.

    As time goes on we'll increasingly develop resources that aid teams in leveraging the results of their voyages. Teams are welcome to return to our ships for subsequent voyages as we carry on our missions

  • Early days

    While we gather the funds to make a major rennovation of our ship, we will engage in mini-projects such as developing our website, a crowdfunding video and the architectural and interior design of the ship. We'll develop a 3D model of what the ship might looks like. Embed this on the website, and explore it in VR.

  • A social enterprise

    We intend to set up as a Community Interest Company in the U.K. We will use our profits to fund technology education where they are most needed, starting with our local community in North Wales. Specifically we want to enable local people to develop technology skills that will support the local economy in the future, create a local culture of innovation and support environmental sustainability.
