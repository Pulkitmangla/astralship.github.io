What is an Astralship?

What drove ancient sailors beyond the horizons in search of new lands? Viking or pirates, when times were tough they took matters into their own hands. We're going to follow their lead.

The Astralship will provide everything a creative team needs to acheive peak performance. Inspiring spaces to focus, build, co-design, create, relax and sleep. When elements of a project are aligned and brought together in the right environment and with the right faciliation, creativity and productivity explode.

Collaboration and Innovation.

We're building a facility where developers, designers, engineers, radical thinkers and all creative beings can work together at peak performance. Where we can channel passion and facilitate collaboration and productivity, creating world-changing innovation through short & focused projects which we call voyages.

A inspiring and exciting space

In the Chapel there is plenty of room to work with. We've build a prototype ship and found it to be an inspirational space.

Innovation is an adventure. We'll create a magical and inspiring interior with high-quality living, working and recreational spaces that suggest adventure on even the most serious and focussed of voyages.

    In the convential paradigm, getting a team together to work on ideas is expensive and time consuming. The big money investors decide on the projects and keep the rewards. By providing a ship which is able to sustain teams at low cost teams can reach prototype stage with optimal efficiency, minimising the time (and funds)the founders need to reach fruition.
