Setting the dreamers free

What drove ancient sailors beyond the horizons in search of new lands? Viking or pirates, when times were tough they took matters into their own hands. We're going to follow their lead.

The Astralship will provide everything a creative team needs to acheive peak performance. Inspiring spaces to sleep, focus, build, co-design, create and relax. When the elements of a project are aligned and brought together in the right environment and with the right faciliation, the results can be a 1000x.

Collaboration and Innovation.

We're building a facility where developers, designers, engineers, radical thinkers and all creatives can work together at peak performance. Channeling passion and creating world-changing innovation through short focussed projects we call voyages.

A inspiring and exciting space

In the Chapel there is plenty of room to work with. We've already build a prototype ship and found to be a very inspiring space though it's quite rough around the edges at the moment. We'll create high-quality living working and recreational spaces. Innovation is an adventure. We'll create a magical and inspiring interior which will suggest adventure on even the most serious of voyages.

Stunning Natural Location

The Chapel stands on the slopes of Elidir across the valley from Snowdon (the highest mountain in England and Wales). It's just a few hundred metres from the boundary of Snowdonia national park.   • Building the ship

    We need an new floor, insulation, ligtning, a wood fired heating system and a beautiful interior. We estimate we need around ¬£50,000 to do a good job of all of this, though we could happily spend ¬£200,000+ if we had the budget. The money will come from our savings, family and friends, and crowding funding. We're developing this plan now but are looking architect, artists psychologists and builders who want to be involved.

  • Sharing the wealth created

    In the convential paradigm, getting a team together to work on ideas is expensive and time consuming. The big money investors decide on the projects and keep the rewards. By providing a ship which is able to sustain teams at low cost we'll be able reach prototype stage very quickly, minimising the cost and time the founders need to invest.

    As time goes on we'll increasingly build resources to help teams leverage the results of their voyages and hope some team will return to our ships for subsequent voyages as they carry on their mission.
