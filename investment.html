Holding company

We intend that the "chapel" (holding company that owns the chapel) will own a portion of equity of each spin-off company - this is to incentivise investors and ensure long-term prosperity of the project.

Kickstarter

We will use a crowdfunding platform. The intention is to obtain extra funding, to conduct real-life market research and make waves in the cyberspace. It is also one of the first voyages for us...

We are still working on the video and exact reward structure. In the meanwhile we are offering super-earliest-bird rewards. The intention is to make use of the traffic and interest even before the Kickstarter is live. The funding collected at this stage will be converted to rewards at 20% discount - this is to incentivise early commitment and take into consideration lack of the platform fees.

(we believe Kickstarter is the best platform because a lot of people have already payment details on file)
