Holding company

We intend that the "chapel" (holding company that owns the chapel) will own a portion of equity of each spin-off company - this is to incentivise investors and ensure long-term prosperity of the project.

We believe that living and working in such an environment will foster innovation on unprecedented levels and our products will be used by people around the world.

Kickstarter

We will use a crowdfunding platform. The intention is to obtain extra cash, to conduct real-life market research and make waves in the cyberspace. It is also a test for us - do we have enough power to promote the project - of course we do but before we put our whole livehood on the line it's always reassuring to have some backing and support

Here are some rewards we were considering.
  • £1 postcard (to get plenty of backers and boost visibility)
  • £5 meal
  • £25 one night stay
  • £100 one hour digital strategy session
  • £500 day of engineering work (consultancy)
